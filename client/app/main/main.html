Fast Food Empire

Run your own food empire!

About the game

  • Goal

    Players compete for influence over the city of Megapolis and various districts in it. They gain influence by upgrading and expanding their own restaurant empire, at first each player starts with one restaurant but can start taking over other food joints once they have the necessary upgrades.

  • Real time

    The game is played in real time. Players gain resources over time whch they use to upgrade their restaurant and hire workers. Each upgrade takes a certain amount of time which increases as the restaurant progresses. Players can hire workers to interfere with their neighbor business.

  • Ending

    The server stops accepting new players after a certain amount of time/players thus allowing a domination victory. Such a victory is achieved when a group of players join into a franchise and once it reaches a dominant percentage of influence in Megapolis.
